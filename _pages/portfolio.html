Northeastern Topology Seminar

I’m co-organizing “Northeastern topology seminar” (“东北拓扑系列活动”) which meets on Tuesdays at 2 pm (unless informed otherwise). The seminar is held in hybrid mode. If you are interested in participarting the seminar or giving a talk, please shoot me an email. The ads of the past talks can be found on http://cos.neu.edu.cn/ (under the section “学术交流”). The other co-organizers are: 陈亮 （东北师大），鄂强（大连海事），李风玲 （大连理工），钟立楠（延边大学）
